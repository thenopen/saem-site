FAQ

What is Saem?

Saem helps you learn Korean from the very beginning — the Hangul alphabet, basic phrases, and on toward TOPIK — using K-pop and K-culture as motivation. It supports explanations in English, 中文, Tiếng Việt, 日本語, Русский, and Oʻzbekcha.

Do I need an account?

No. Saem has no sign-up or login. Your progress is stored only on your device.

Does Saem work offline?

Yes. All lessons, images, and pronunciation work without a network connection.

Why is there no sound on the simulator?

Pronunciation uses the device's built-in text-to-speech and works on real iPhones. Some simulators have a known voice-catalog issue that produces no audio.

How is my data handled?

Saem collects only anonymous usage statistics (no personal identity, no tracking). See our Privacy Policy.
